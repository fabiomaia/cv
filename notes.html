Taking notes Note-taking applications have too many features. I just want to write stuff down quickly. Like a thought dump, a stream of random strings. For me this suffices 
#!/bin/sh
vim stream.txt
Then you can get creative and do things like, e.g., https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=23889370 
#!/bin/sh
read _text
_date=$(date +'%Y-%m-%d')
echo -e "\n$_date: $_text" >> ~/Dropbox/notes/stream.txt
